an apology

Sorry for not bringing
you flowers.

Not because I forgot how I feel. I never do. I got so excited just to see you and to hear your voice that everything else, including the flowers I wished to carry for you, slipped right past me that day.

I wish every flower I meant to bring you that day could still find its way to you, carried by all my love for you, my wife.

things I think and don't always say

You are, quietly, always,
the best part of my life

I do not draw everyone I see. But the moment I saw you, something made me want to draw you so badly that I could not even explain it to myself. I am afraid that what artists call the Artists Curse might happen to you.

My heart yearned for you so much after sixteen hours of travel that I could not spend one more moment without you. I rushed straight to you. I am sorry that I did not bring the flowers I had wished to bring.

Every second of every day I am thinking about you. Wherever I go, if I see a flower, the first thing that comes to my mind is your face. I have bought so many flower plants just to remember you.

I will forget many things, but never the things you like and love. I am sorry if I ever made you feel like I forget the little things about you.
